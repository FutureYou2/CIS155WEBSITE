What is The FINALS?

The Finals is a competitive multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Embark Studios. The game is presented as a futuristic virtual game show where contestants compete in destructive arenas for fame, sponsorships, and prize money. Instead of taking place in a traditional military or battlefield setting, the action occurs inside simulated arenas that resemble real-world locations but are designed for entertainment and spectacle. In this world, contestants enter a broadcasted competition where every match is treated like a televised sporting event. Commentators narrate the action while virtual audiences watch competitors battle across highly detailed arenas. The environment is an important part of the show, with entire buildings, walls, and structures capable of being destroyed during the match. Each arena is designed with vertical spaces, interior rooms, and destructible terrain that can dramatically change as players fight. Skyscrapers can collapse, walls can be blown open, and floors can be destroyed to create new paths through the map. Because the competition takes place in a simulated environment, the arenas can resemble different cities or architectural styles while still being optimized for intense, fast-paced gameplay. The Finals overall is known for having:

  • Fast paced gameplay
  • Destructible environments
  • Unique classes

What is the gameplay like?

The gameplay of THE FINALS centers on fast-paced team-based matches where squads compete to complete objectives while fighting rival teams. Players choose from different contestant builds—typically categorized as light, medium, or heavy—each with different mobility, durability, and equipment options. A key mechanic of the game is its extensive environmental destruction system. Players can use explosives, heavy weapons, and gadgets to tear apart buildings and reshape the battlefield. This allows teams to create shortcuts, destroy enemy cover, or collapse entire structures to disrupt opponents. The most common objective mode involves teams locating vaults containing cash, transporting them to a cash-out station, and defending that location while the money is deposited. Other teams can attempt to steal the cash-out during the process, leading to chaotic multi-team fights around key objectives. Players also use specialized gadgets and abilities such as grappling hooks, jump pads, barricades, cloaking devices, and healing equipment. These tools allow teams to approach objectives creatively, whether by quickly traversing the map, defending a position, or ambushing opponents. Matches reward strategic teamwork and quick decision-making. Teams must balance aggressive combat with careful objective control while adapting to constantly changing environments caused by destruction. The combination of high mobility, destructible maps, and competitive objectives creates a dynamic experience where no two matches play out exactly the same.
